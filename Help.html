Frequently Asked Questions

Tickets can be purchased online through our official website, via the phone booking line, or in person at the Etihad Stadium ticket office. Cityzens members get priority access to tickets.

Cityzens membership offers priority ticket access, exclusive content, discounts on merchandise, opportunities to engage with the club, and access to member-only events.

The Etihad Stadium is easily accessible by tram, bus, or car. We recommend using public transport on match days. The stadium is served by the Metrolink (Etihad Campus stop) and multiple bus routes. Detailed directions are available on our website.
